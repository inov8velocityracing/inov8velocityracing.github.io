Inov8 Velocity Racing

Who We Are

We are Inov8 Velocity Racing, a Hydrogen R/C car team competing in the H2GP competition. Our goal is to push the boundaries of sustainable energy through innovative engineering and teamwork.

What We Do

The H2GP competition challenges teams to design, build, and optimize hydrogen-powered R/C cars. Our team works collaboratively to solve engineering challenges, develop creative solutions, and race toward a more sustainable future.
